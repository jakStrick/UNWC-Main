Board Member Volunteers

We are currently looking for board members. Do you have a desire to serve your community? A vested interest in watershed health? Or perhaps a skill set you have been longing to utilize? Our board volunteers are the lifeblood of the UNWC and we value diversity of skills and perspectives. No matter your background or experience, we have a place for you. Project Volunteers

The key ingredient to a successful project is the project volunteer. Whether planting trees, working in the native plant nursery, or helping at an event, these individuals are the boots on the ground who make it happen. Do you enjoy getting your hands dirty and trekking through the forest? Are you more of a "behind the scenes" person who finds satisfaction in a job well done? If so, consider becoming a project volunteer. Contact us today to find out how.

Youth Involvement

Kids and teens can be involved! The UNWC has special projects and internships designed specifically for young people to educate the next generation about the importance of watershed health. Contact us for more information.
